FIRST Alumni Mexico is a group of undergraduate students and professionals from different places across Mexico.

We organize events and workshops that help teams develop their programming, mechanics and electronics skills, but also in other areas such as project management, media and communications, community service and more!

We help other members in advancing their career, finding job opportunities and taking advantage of the opportunities offered by FIRST to their alumni.
